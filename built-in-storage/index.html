FAQ

Built-in storage questions.

Do I need exact measurements before getting in touch?

No. Rough dimensions and a few photos are enough to start. Exact measurements come later during measure-up.

Can I get a ballpark from photos?

Usually, yes. Photos are not a final quote, but they are enough to see whether the job looks like a fit.

Can you build into awkward spaces?

Yes. Custom cabinetry is often best for odd gaps, hallway spaces, alcoves and areas where standard furniture does not fit properly.

Can you make it look like it belongs in the room?

That is the goal. The cabinetry is designed around the space so it feels intentional, not like furniture pushed against a wall.

Do you do one-off shelves?

Cabinet Ninja is best suited to proper built-in cabinetry and storage projects, not small one-off shelf jobs.

Are painting, flooring or wall repairs included?

Not automatically. If extra finishing work is needed, it will be discussed and clearly listed in the quote scope.

Do you install the cabinetry?

Yes. Cabinet Ninja designs, builds and installs the cabinetry.

What areas do you service?

Rotorua, Taupō, Tokoroa, Atiamuri, Reporoa, South Waikato and surrounding areas.
