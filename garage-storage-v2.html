Questions people usually have

Do I need to know exactly what I want before enquiring?

No. Most people don’t. Photos, rough measurements, and a basic idea of what annoys you about the space is enough to start.

Can you work around stuff already in the garage?

Yes. We can design around things like access doors, freezers, service points, and how you actually use the room.

Are those package prices fixed?

No. They’re starting-point guide prices so people can self-qualify. Final pricing depends on layout, size, and features.

Do you only do garages?

Garages are one of our key offers, but if someone also needs wardrobes or other cabinetry, that can be discussed separately.
