The Problem

Most garages don’t need more space. They need better storage.

Garages often end up carrying the overflow for the whole house, while still needing to work as a garage.

  • Tools and gear spread across every spare surface
  • Plastic tubs stacked against walls
  • Cleaning products and laundry overflow with no proper home
  • Freezers, washers, dryers or appliances sitting awkwardly in the way
  • No useful bench space for sorting, folding or working
  • A garage that still needs room for parking, storage and day-to-day use

FAQ

Garage storage questions.

Can you work around freezers, washers or dryers?

Yes. Send a photo showing the appliance positions and rough wall width so the first conversation starts in the right place.

Do I need exact measurements before getting in touch?

No. Rough dimensions are fine to start. Exact measurements come later during measure-up.

Can I get a ballpark from photos?

Usually, yes. Photos are not a final quote, but they are enough to see whether the job looks like a fit.

Do you install the cabinetry?

Yes. Cabinet Ninja designs, builds and installs the cabinetry.

Do you install flat-pack garage storage?

No. Cabinet Ninja builds custom cabinetry to suit the room, the storage problem and the way you use the space.
