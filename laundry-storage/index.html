FAQ

Laundry storage questions.

Do I need exact measurements before getting in touch?

No. Rough dimensions and a few photos are enough to start. Exact measurements come later during measure-up.

Can I get a ballpark from photos?

Usually, yes. Photos are not a final quote, but they are enough to see whether the job looks like a fit.

Can you work around washers, dryers or freezers?

Yes. Send photos showing the appliance positions and rough dimensions so the first ballpark starts in the right place.

Can you add bench space over appliances?

Usually, yes, depending on the appliance layout, clearances and how the space is built.

Are plumbing and electrical included?

Not automatically. If plumbing or electrical work is needed, it will be discussed and clearly listed in the quote scope.

Do you install the cabinetry?

Yes. Cabinet Ninja designs, builds and installs the cabinetry.

What areas do you service?
